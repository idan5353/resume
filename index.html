My Road to Achievement🎓

From being a combat soldier to becoming an AWS Certified Solutions Architect, this is my journey into the high-tech world. With the help of the "Atidim" organization and my studies at Kernelios, I was able to transform my career path and skills. Here's a glimpse into the milestones of my journey.



Atidim: Bridging the Gap

Through the Atidim organization, I had the opportunity to transition from a combat soldier into the world of high-tech. Atidim specializes in helping combat soldiers, like me, enter the tech industry by providing mentorship, training, and job placement opportunities.

5 Months at Kernelios

During my 5 months at Kernelios College, I gained deep knowledge in cloud computing, infrastructure automation, and DevOps practices. The program was intense, but it provided me with hands-on experience that made a significant impact on my career. I completed the course with the AWS Solutions Architect certification, a key step in my journey.

AWS Solutions Architect Certification

Completing the AWS Solutions Architect certification was a significant milestone. It not only deepened my understanding of cloud infrastructure but also validated my skills in designing and deploying scalable and reliable systems using AWS services.
